about hug a tree and the hugging gang

Tom Robbins once wrote: I need to babble with a brook or two, inhale starlight, make friends with some trees. Hug a Tree helps the 'stadteinsamen' people of Vancouver, BC make friends with all sorts of trees and keep track of them. So facebook for people and trees? Yes.

The hugging gang is made up of Curtis Hermanson, Alexis Jensen, Wenny Jiang and Joy Zhao. We are undergrads of UBC in the Computer Science department tasked with building a web application for our software engineering class. The datasets for this project were provided by the City of Vancouver.

Happy hugging.
